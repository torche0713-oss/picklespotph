Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a court?

Click "Add Court" on the home page and fill out the form. Courts are approved automatically.

How do I book a court?

Click a court on the map or in the list view, then use the "Book Now" button to send an inquiry to the owner.

Is PickleSpotPH free?

Basic use is free. Court owners can upgrade to Pro (₱399/month) for featured listings, unlimited photos, and analytics.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Log in to your dashboard and click "Upgrade to Pro" under your plan badge. Follow the GCash payment instructions.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from the dashboard. Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period.
